Children’s Ministry

We provide Bible-based teaching, fun activities, and care for children during our services. Our goal is to help children grow in faith and love for God.

Building strong, godly men through teaching, fellowship, and community service projects.

Community Outreach

Serving our neighborhood through evangelism, and helping the needy.
